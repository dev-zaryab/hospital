Speciality General Surgery

Dr. M. B. Tayade


Presently working as the Head of the Department of General Surgery & Postgraduate Vice Dean Has a Teaching experience more than 25 years in the field.

He is an excellent Clinician & a natural Surgeon & has been training postgraduate students in the field of gastrointestinal & laparoscopic Surgery.

Dr. Tayade is Working as Chairman & Member to various Committees in the University & Institution pertaining to the University examinations & Purchase of Instruments & Infrastructure.

Instrumental in organisation & Implementation of various diagnostic & Operative camps in the rural regions of Maharashtra.

Has been actively organizing various Live Hands on Operative Workshops & Conferences , functioning as Executive member to various governing bodies.

Research is his field of keen interest & has more than 50 Publications National & International to his names.
