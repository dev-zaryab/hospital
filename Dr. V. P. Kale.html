Dr. V. P. Kale


1. Working as Faculty since 19 Jan 1996 till today as Asst Professor, Associate Professor and Professor. Total teaching experience is almost 19 years.

2. Participated in Outdoor Medical Camps at Nadurbar and Jalgaon district during the period 2011-2013.

3. Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik, PG teacher and guide since last 12 years. Provided guidance to near about 16 PG students.

4. Working as a resource person to MUHS Nashik Various Committees related to Psychiatry subject as staff selection, examiner, subject expert, etc.

5. Working as Mental Health Expert for District Mental Disability Board Mumbai, Standing Medical Board in Medico-Legal cases.

6. Working as a member to assist administration in Grant Government Medical College on various committees.

7. Working as a Vice Dean Undergraduate section since Nov 2014.
