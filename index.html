History JJ Hospital

The Grant Government medical College, Mumbai is one
of the premiere institution of India affiliated to the
Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik. The
Foundation of the institution dates back to 1845 and
with a consistent performance in ranking in the top 10
Medical colleges all over India.Sir JJ Group of Hospitals
is a conglomerate of 4 Hospitals : Sir JJ Hospital,
St.Georges Hospital, Gokuldas Tejpal Hospital & Cama
Albless Hospital.
