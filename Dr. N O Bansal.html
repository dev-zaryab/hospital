Dr. N O Bansal


Professor and Head of Cardiology Department, Grant Medical College and Sir JJ Group of Hospitals. Have been working at Dept. of Cardiology, J.J.Hospital since1984 in various capacities and designations. I have treated more than 1 lakh patients of all kinds of Cardio Vascular diseases in smallest of the new born Kids to the most elderly( greater than 90 Yrs of age),both on out patient and Indoor basis. I have carried out all the routine and highly specialized procedures running into several thousands, namely Echo Doppler Studies, Stress testing, Temporary Pacing, Permanent Pacing, incl.ICD, HF device(>750). Angiographies (>40000) Angioplasties of all types incl. PAMI, Unstable Angina, Cardiogenic Shock (>15000). Balloon Mitral and other Valvuloplasties(>5000).
